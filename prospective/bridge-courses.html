Bridge Courses

Mathematics Bridge Course at the Mathematical Institute

The Course is aimed at the beginners in the Bachelor's and grammar school teaching courses at the Mathematical Institute. It happens approximately 1 month before the start of lecture period. Registration is required.

Math Crash Course for Physicists

In the Math Crash Course, the entire contents of school math are repeated. It is aimed at first-year students of the Bachelor's degree in Physics and Physics plus Meteorology and happens approximately 2 weeks before the start of lecture period. Registration is required.
