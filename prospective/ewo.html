First Semester Weekend (EWO)

The First Semester Weekend is an offer from the GAF for first year students.

Date: 10/17/2025 until 10/19/2025

You can get to know fellow students and your student council there in a relaxed atmosphere for a weekend, and get an insight into the student council work through games and working groups. If you are interested in participating, you will receive all further information on the program and registration at the O-Phase.
