Trial Studies

During the summer holidays, the LMU offers trial studies in which you can get a good insight into what it looks like to study here.

Trial Studies in Computer Science

The trial course of the Institute of Computer Science takes place every year on the last Friday before the Easter holidays until the first Wednesday of the holiday. The course is aimed at students from the 10th grade. Registration is required.

Trial Studies in Mathematics (LMU-Math-Summer)

The trial course at the Mathematical Institute is organized at the beginning of September. The course is aimed at students in the last school years before graduation. Registration is required.

Trial Studies in Physics

The trial course of the Faculty of Physics is aimed at 11th grade students and particularly interested 10th grade students. Registration is required.
